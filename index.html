Drop Me a Line

I moved to Raleigh, N.C. in 2005.

Not sure what to do with myself, and being very shy, I decided to enroll in art classes at Sertoma Art Center in 2007.

I started with basic drawing classes, then took classes in colored pencil and fell in love.

I learned different levels of what you can do with them. I've taken several classes since, and joined art groups where I meet up with other artists.

I'm continually learning

at a very enjoyable level. I find art is a place where I can go and get away from life. I find it to very therapeutic.

I still use Sertoma to explore different mediums.

And I try to sign up for workshops whenever I'm able to do so.
